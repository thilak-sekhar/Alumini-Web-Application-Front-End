OFFICIAL NEWS LETTER

The Apollo Knowledge City recently launched its first campus newsletter, *The Apollo Campus Cornet*, during an event on October 26, 2024, held on Freshers' Day. This newsletter aims to connect the university community by celebrating achievements and sharing stories. It was created as a platform for student expression, with over 70% of its content contributed by students, including articles, stories, and poems. University leaders such as Vice-Chancellor Dr. H. Vinod Bhat, Registrar Prof. M. Potharaju, and various faculty members attended the launch. The newsletter is available in both print and digital formats, marking the start of a tradition that fosters creativity and unity within the Apollo Knowledge City community.
